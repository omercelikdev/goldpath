# Upgrading to 0.1.0-preview.3 (train accumulating — not yet released)

## Behavioral break: admin surfaces are tenant-scoped (admin-contract revision R1)

On apps with `multiTenancy: true`, the `/goldpath/admin/*` surfaces now scope every read
and verb to the AMBIENT tenant (same resolution as your business endpoints). What changes
for you:

- Operators who previously relied on `?tenant=` (or the implicit all-tenants view) need
  the new `goldpath-ops-all-tenants` role (configurable: `Goldpath:Auth:OpsAllTenantsRole`)
  on top of `goldpath-ops`. Without it: `403` on foreign tenants, ambient scope otherwise.
- Requests that reach the admin head WITHOUT tenant resolution now answer `400` instead
  of returning every tenant's rows (fail-closed).
- The campaign surface demands the cross-tenant role outright on multi-tenant apps (its
  rows carry no tenant column — it is cross-tenant by nature).
- Single-tenant apps (`multiTenancy: false`): no change.

Routes, nouns, envelopes and paging are unchanged — console/scripts keep working once the
operator carries the right role.
